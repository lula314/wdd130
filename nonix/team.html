Meet the Team

Lucy

Lucy helps oversee and direct the entire design process from character design and storyboarding to writing and programming. She tends to focus on the technical side of things, writing the code that makes the games run and making sure everything is working properly as far as programming goes.

Ty

Ty also directs and oversees the entire process within the designing and creating of a game. He focuses more on the creative side of the finished product by supplying all of the amazing artwork as well as most of the voiceovers found within the games.

Daisha

Daisha focuses on the behind the scenes work, she develops the amazing characters and thrilling storylines of the games. She carries each idea from napkin doodles to fully fledged storyboards ensuring a captivating story in each and every game.
